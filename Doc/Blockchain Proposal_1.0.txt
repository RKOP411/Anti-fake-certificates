AI Validation + NFT-based certificates + Hybrid Blockchain

University → [Send NFT Cert: CertificateID, StudentName, IssuerID, Date...] → Email
↓  
Student → [Logs in via Phone Number/ Email] → Uploads Token(CertificateID)+ Certificate Image(NFT Cert)
↓  
AI Engine → [Checks Name/School + Detects Forgery] → Valid? → Records on Blockchain  
↓  
Employer → [Queries Blockchain] → Gets Trusted Verification
